Lets stick to one naming convention... 

variables: 
-start with a lower-case letter 
-do not contain underscores (except as a prefix) 
-use an underscore as a prefix for private and protected variables (not for public) 
-static finals all in CAPS 
e.g: 
correct: 

private _thisIsAnExample 

public thisIsAnExample 
wrong: 

protected This_is_wrong 

public _thisIsWrongToo 

methods: 
-start with a lower-case letter 
-do not contain underscores
